About us

We participate in industrializing and establishing a collective global economy. By creating opportunities, employments and providing excellent services to our customers with integrity and competence. We are quite optimistic that our values and the quality of the Services we offer will help us drive our business to enviable heights and also help us attract the number of clients that will make the business profitable. We are a company that is dedicated to establish good business relationship with our clients by giving them value for services as well as reasons for them to hire our services over and over again. It is with no doubt that our excellent customer services and the quality of services we offer makes us welcome repeated customers and handle massive deals both from Government agencies and Industrial giants. Our client’s best interest is always our first priority, and everything we do is guided by our values and professional ethics. We ensure that we hold ourselves accountable to the highest standards by meeting our client’s needs precisely and completely. We cultivate a working environment that provides a human, sustainable approach to earning a living, and living in our world, for our partners, employees and for our clients.

Objectives

  • To enhance development of the industrialized society by the introduction of a new technology for protection of the Ozone layer.
  • To introduce plant gas from plants which is to be used in all machines including cars, heavy machines and planes.
  • To construct a manufacturing industry for the production of plant gas which will help in cleaning and combating carbon dioxide from the ozone layer and help in the rehabilitation and rejuvenation of the destructed layer.
  • Establishment of agricultural scheme/plantations for growing those plants and other agricultural products.
  • Establishment of motor vehicle manufacturing industry which will use and apply the said plant gas.
  • Introduction of human and animal (livestock) medicine and vaccine manufacturing industry.
  • Establishment of schools and colleges dealing with designing, innovation and creativity studies.
  • Establishment of livestock farming (ranches).
  • Establishment of Construction Company and agency for construction of buildings, roads, and gardens.
  • Establishment of Transportation Company for civilians, for both local and international voyages.

VISION

Is to become one of the preferred choices of individual and organization when it comes to the demand for supply of various industrial services ranging from machines, construction, designing, agricultural, health, transportation and logistics in the whole of the United Republic of Tanzania and the world.

MISSION

Is to build a successful industrialized Tanzania and to other nations of the world from small, medium and large industry. We are a company that will operate in the whole of the United Republic of Tanzania and to other nations of the world; a company that will grow to be listed amongst the top services supplier in the industrialized global economy.
